Start here

New to RallyEdge? Do these four things first.

You'll be up and running in under 10 minutes — and the app will already feel personal.

1
👤

Set up your Profile

Add your USTA level, play style, dominant hand, and Pep Talk tone. This is what makes everything feel personal.

2
📔

Log a session

Log a recent match, lesson, or practice. Your stats update instantly and your history starts building.

3
🎯

Add a goal

Add one thing you're actively working on. Be specific — the more detail you add, the better your AI Goal Tip will be.

4
🔍

Scout a few opponents

Add two or three players you face regularly. Tag their strengths and weaknesses and add any notes you have on their game.

📔
Journal

Log every session

1

Tap the + button to log a session

From the Journal tab or the Home screen quick actions, tap + to start a new entry. Choose between Match, Lesson, or Practice.

2

Log a Match

Record your opponent, score, surface, format (singles or doubles), result, and any notes on what worked and what was challenging. You can link opponents directly to your Scout profiles.

Scheduled upcoming matches can be logged in advance and converted to played matches after the fact.

3

Log a Lesson

Record your coach's name, the skills you worked on, coaching tips (one per line), your rating, and any notes. Coaching tips you log here feed directly into your AI Pep Talk — so the more specific the better.

Tip: Log coaching tips exactly as your coach said them. The AI uses them verbatim when generating your pre-match pep talk.
4

Log a Practice

Track practice sessions with location, surface, duration, other players on court, and which of your active goals you worked on. Linking goals to practice sessions helps you track real progress over time.

5

View and edit past sessions

Your Journal shows two sections — Upcoming and Past Sessions. Tap any entry to view the full detail. Tap Edit Entry to make changes. Use the filter pills (All / Match / Lesson / Practice) to narrow the list.

🔍
Scout

Know your opponents

1

Add an opponent

Tap the + button in the Scout tab to add a new opponent. Enter their name, club, USTA level, dominant hand, and select their strengths and weaknesses from the tag lists.

2

Add scouting notes

Use the Notes field for anything that doesn't fit the tags — their tendencies at net, how they respond to pace, how they play under pressure. The more detail you add, the better your AI Game Plan will be.

Tip: Scouting notes feed directly into the AI Game Plan. Specific notes produce specific, actionable game plans.
3

Link opponents to matches

When logging a match, search for your opponent by name to link them to your Scout profile. Linked matches build your head-to-head record automatically — visible on the opponent's detail screen.

4

Review head-to-head history

Tap any opponent to see their full profile, your win-loss record against them, and a breakdown of linked matches. Use the search bar to find opponents quickly across name, club, or notes.

🎯
Goals

Improve with intention

1

Create a goal

Tap + to add a new goal. Give it a title, choose a category (Technique, Strategy, Mental, or Fitness), write a description of what you're working on, and set your starting progress.

Tip: The more specific your goal description, the better your AI Goal Tip will be. "Improve my serve" produces a generic tip. "My second serve breaks down under pressure — I double fault at the worst moments" produces a concrete, actionable drill.
2

Update your progress

Tap Edit on any goal to update your progress percentage and status. Goals can be Active, Paused, or Achieved. The Goals tab shows collapsible sections for each status so you can stay focused on what matters now.

3

Link goals to practice sessions

When logging a Lesson or Practice, select which goals you worked on in that session. This keeps your goal progress connected to your actual court time.

🎒
Gear

Your equipment locker

1

Add your gear

Tap + to add a racket, shoes, or accessory. Each gear type has its own relevant fields — rackets include string tension, grip size, and role (Main or Backup). Shoes include size, color, and fit notes.

2

Star your main racket

Mark your primary racket as Main to keep it at the top of your gear list. You can have multiple rackets and toggle their role anytime.

3

Track purchase dates

Add a purchase date to each item so you always know when your strings were last changed or when it's time to replace your shoes.

🏠
Home

Your season at a glance

1

Stats strip

The four cards at the top of Home show your live season stats — total sessions logged, win/loss record, total court time, and number of active goals. These update automatically every time you log a session.

2

Upcoming match banner

If you have a scheduled match in your Journal, it appears as a banner on Home. Tap it to view details or generate your AI Game Plan for that opponent.

3

Latest coach lesson

Your most recent lesson's coaching tips appear on Home so they're always front of mind before you head to the court.

4

Watch & Learn

A curated strip of short coaching videos from FuzzyYellowBalls — all under 4 minutes. Videos refresh monthly. Tap any video to watch it on YouTube.

🤖
AI Features

Personalized coaching, powered by your game

1

AI Pep Talk

Before a match, tap Get My Pep Talk on the Home screen. RallyEdge generates a personalized pre-match message built around your recent sessions, your active goals, your coach's latest tips, and your upcoming opponent — all in the tone you set in your Profile.

How to get the best pep talk: Keep your Journal up to date, log your coach's tips after every lesson, and make sure your upcoming match is scheduled in the Journal before you generate.
2

AI Game Plan

When you save a new opponent in Scout, RallyEdge automatically generates a 3-sentence game plan tailored to that opponent's strengths and weaknesses. Find it on the opponent's detail screen. You can refresh it anytime if you've updated their scouting notes.

3

AI Goal Tips

When you create a new goal, RallyEdge generates a 2-sentence coaching tip — a specific drill to try at your next practice, and a cue for applying it under match pressure. Find it on the goal's detail screen. You can refresh it anytime.

⚙️
Profile

Customize your experience

1

Access your Profile

Tap the avatar circle in the top right of the Home screen, or tap Menu from any screen. Your Profile panel slides up from the bottom.

2

Set your Pep Talk preferences

Choose your preferred tone (Calm, Fierce, Funny, or Spiritual) and how often you want pep talks generated. You can also set a pump-up song from your iPhone music library — it plays while your pep talk appears.

3

Update your tennis profile

Keep your USTA level, play style, dominant hand, and preferred surface up to date. This information feeds into every AI feature — more accurate profile data means more relevant pep talks and game plans.

4

Manage your account

Tap Menu → Account to view your subscription plan, change your password, and manage your account settings.
